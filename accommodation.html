Accommodation in Sarawak

Hotels

Grand Kuching Hotel

A luxurious hotel offering modern amenities.

Nearby Attractions: Sarawak Museum, Waterfront Esplanade.
Kuching City Center

Pullman Kuching

A luxurious 5-star hotel in the heart of Kuching city, Pullman offers spacious rooms with modern decor, excellent dining options, and panoramic views of the city.

Nearby Attractions: Kuching Waterfront, Sarawak Museum, Main Bazaar.
Jalan Mathies, Kuching City Center.

The Waterfront Hotel

A contemporary hotel offering comfort and convenience with direct access to the Plaza Merdeka shopping center. It features an infinity pool overlooking the Sarawak River.

Nearby Attractions: Old Courthouse, Sarawak State Legislative Assembly, Brooke Gallery at Fort Margherita.
Kuching Waterfront, Jalan Tun Abang Haji Openg.

Hilton Kuching

Located along the Sarawak River, Hilton Kuching provides world-class amenities, river-view rooms, and a prime location close to the city's attractions.

Nearby Attractions: Darul Hana Bridge, Tua Pek Kong Temple, Carpenter Street (Chinatown).
Jalan Tunku Abdul Rahman, Kuching Waterfront.
Homestays

Annah Rais Longhouse Homestay

Experience traditional Bidayuh longhouse living in a serene village surrounded by lush greenery. This homestay offers cultural activities like bamboo cooking and traditional dances.

Nearby Attractions: Annah Rais Hot Springs, Borneo Highlands, Jungle trekking routes.
Annah Rais Village, about 60 km from Kuching City.

Matang Homestay

A perfect blend of nature and comfort, this homestay is near the lush landscapes of Kubah National Park. Guests can enjoy bird watching, jungle trekking, and visits to nearby waterfalls.

Nearby Attractions: Kubah National Park, Matang Wildlife Centre, Mount Santubong.
Matang, Kuching.

Nanga Sumpa Homestay

Deep in the rainforest, this homestay provides a remote experience with an Iban community. Guests can enjoy longboat rides, wildlife spotting, and visits to nearby waterfalls.

Nearby Attractions: Batang Ai National Park, Enseluai Waterfall, Iban cultural immersion.
Batang Ai, about 250 km from Kuching.

Kampung Benuk Homestay

Located in one of Sarawak's oldest Bidayuh villages, this homestay provides an immersive cultural experience, including handicraft workshops and local cooking lessons.

Nearby Attractions: Wind Cave Nature Reserve, Fairy Cave, Serikin Weekend Market.
34 km from Kuching, easily accessible by road.
Camping

Kubah National Park Campsite

Located just outside Kuching, this campsite is perfect for hikers and wildlife enthusiasts. It offers basic camping facilities and is surrounded by lush rainforest and scenic waterfalls.

Nearby Attractions: Frog Pond, Waterfall Trail, Matang Wildlife Centre.
Kubah National Park, 20 km from Kuching.

Bako National Park Campsite

This iconic national park offers a unique camping experience with the chance to spot proboscis monkeys, bearded pigs, and stunning coastal rock formations. Campsites are basic but offer a rare opportunity to immerse in nature.

Nearby Attractions: Telok Pandan Kecil Beach, Unique rock formations, Mangrove trails.
Accessible by boat from Kampung Bako (about 37 km from Kuching).

Sematan Palm Beach Resort Campsite

Though primarily a resort, Sematan also offers camping spots right by the beach, providing stunning ocean views and a relaxing seaside atmosphere.

Nearby Attractions: Talang-Satang National Park, Sebat Waterfall, Fishing and kayaking activities.
Sematan, approximately 2 hours’ drive from Kuching.

Gunung Gading National Park Campsite

Perfect for adventurers, this park is home to the world’s largest flower, the Rafflesia. The campsite offers serene surroundings and access to jungle trekking trails.

Nearby Attractions: Rafflesia Blooming Sites (seasonal), Waterfalls and hiking trails, Lundu Town.
Gunung Gading National Park, about 2 hours’ drive from Kuching.
