Explore the Best Places in Sarawak

Top Destinations

Kuching City

Must-visit spots: Sarawak Cultural Village, Kuching Waterfront, Borneo Cultures Museum.

Mulu National Park

Explore the world-renowned caves like Deer Cave and Sarawak Chamber. Don't miss the bat exodus at dusk.

Bako National Park

Known for its proboscis monkeys, mangroves, and serene beaches.

Hidden Gems

Siniawan Night Market

A historic street turned night market with food, music, and charm.

Santubong Peninsula

Discover Mount Santubong, waterfalls, and dolphin-spotting boat rides.

Seasonal Spots

Rainforest World Music Festival

Held every July, this festival brings global music and local culture together.

Fruit Season

From July to September, enjoy exotic fruits like durian and mangosteen.

Romantic Getaways

Damai Beach Resort

Perfect for sunset walks, spa days, and romantic dinners.

Permai Rainforest Resort

Stay in treehouses surrounded by lush greenery.
