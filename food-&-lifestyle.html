Food & Lifestyle in Sarawak

Local Food

Laksa Sarawak

A spicy, coconut-based noodle soup packed with prawns, shredded chicken, and bean sprouts.

Kolo Mee

Flavorful noodles with minced pork, char siu, and scallions in a light soy sauce.

Manok Pansoh

Chicken cooked in bamboo with aromatic herbs, a traditional Dayak dish.

Umai

A Melanau specialty, this raw fish salad is marinated with lime juice, onions, and chilies.

Cafes

Rustic Cafes

Cozy, aesthetically pleasing cafes perfect for relaxing with local brews.

Hipster Spots

Trendy cafes offering creative drinks and Instagrammable interiors.

Street Food

Satay

Grilled meat skewers served with spicy peanut sauce.

Rojak

A local salad mix of fruits, vegetables, and a tangy peanut dressing.

Lifestyle Experiences

Kampung Stays

Immerse yourself in the traditional lifestyle of Sarawakian villages.

Bidayuh Longhouse Visits

Discover the culture and history of Sarawak's indigenous communities.
