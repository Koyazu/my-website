Discover Sarawak: Where Adventure Meets Culture

Explore thrilling adventures, rich traditions, vibrant city life, and exotic wildlife.

Adventure Activities

Caving at Mulu National Park (Miri)

Explore the world-renowned caves of Mulu, including the Sarawak Chamber and Deer Cave.

Hiking at Mount Santubong (Kuching)

Challenge yourself with steep climbs and breathtaking views of the rainforest and coastline.

White-Water Rafting (Padawan River)

Navigate thrilling rapids while soaking in the lush greenery of Sarawak.

Cultural Experiences

Sarawak Cultural Village (Kuching)

Step into Sarawak's living museum and experience the traditions of its ethnic groups.

Iban Longhouse Stay (Batang Ai)

Immerse yourself in Iban culture with storytelling, dances, and local feasts.

City Life

Kuching Waterfront

Take a leisurely stroll along the picturesque Sarawak River and enjoy vibrant street life.

Carpenter Street

Discover quirky cafes, street food, and bustling night markets in the heart of Kuching.
