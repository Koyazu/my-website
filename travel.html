Travel to Sarawak

Getting Here

Sarawak is well-connected by air and sea. Major international airports like Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA) and Singapore Changi Airport provide flights to Sarawak’s airports in Kuching, Miri, and Sibu. Domestic travelers can also fly from Peninsular Malaysia, Sabah, and other parts of Borneo. Alternatively, ferry services are available for those seeking a scenic sea journey.

Transportation

Exploring Sarawak is made easy with various transportation options. For urban areas, taxis and ride-hailing services like Grab are widely available. To reach rural areas or national parks, car rentals or guided tours are recommended. Public buses connect major towns, and riverboats offer a unique way to explore Sarawak's waterways.

Travel Tips

  • Pack Light: Bring breathable clothing, as Sarawak has a tropical climate with high humidity.
  • Stay Protected: Use insect repellent and sunscreen, especially if visiting rainforests or national parks.
  • Cash & Cards: While cards are accepted in urban areas, carry cash for rural areas and markets.
  • Learn a Few Words: Knowing basic Bahasa Malaysia phrases can enhance your experience.

Travel Packages
